My goal is to partner with authors who are passionate about their stories. Writing is a shared love of both the editor and the writer. As such, I look forward to navigating this challenging, yet rewarding experience with you. I specialize in Young Adult fiction, as well as any other kind of murder mystery fiction, but feel free to send me a sample of your work if you think I would be a good fit for your piece. Manuscripts larger than 100,000 words will be taken on a case by case basis.

Content Edit

  • Feedback on completeness of plot
  • Feedback on character motivations and believability
  • Suggestions of strategies to implement in writing
  • Positive encouragement on working elements

$0.0075 cents/word up to 100,000 words. Example: 100,000 words = $750; 50,000 words = $375.

Copy Edit

  • Sentence by sentence revision
  • Grammatical corrections
  • Feedback on consistency of phrasing and word usage

$0.005 cents/word up to the first 100,000 words. Example: 100,000 words = $500; 50,000 words = $250.

Proofread

  • Final review
  • Polishing touches on word usage
  • Final read through and feedback

Flat rate of $99 up to 100,000 words.

Custom Editing Package

For additional combinations of any of the above services please contact me at the information listed below and I'm certain we can work out a deal we will both be pleased with.

What to expect

I ask all authors to submit a seven page sample of their novel before I will take them as a client. I will then email back a timeframe of when you can expect the manuscript to be finished.

Payment

All payments are processed through PayPal where you can pay with your own personal account, credit card, or debit card.
