Allisyn Ma

Allisyn Ma (Edmonds) graduated from Western Washington University (WWU) in English with an emphasis in creative writing. While in school she took classes in editing and publishing and was selected as the example publishing piece from her class. While at WWU she spent two years working with collegiate writers at WWU’s writing center. Here she was trained in the writing center’s pedagogy for assisting students in the processes of writing original academic pieces. Throughout her time working there she facilitated multiple one-on-one workshops daily using her research on writing and editing tools to aid the writer.

She was published in the WWU's independent magazine, Inkspeak 2012, for her piece "Swiss Cheese and Jesus".

She continued her editing experience by journeying abroad to Santiago, Chile where she worked as an editor for the news source, magazine, and radio broadcast I Love Chile News. It is the most widely read English news source in Chile and she edited their online content as well as worked with international writers to produce original and engaging content.
